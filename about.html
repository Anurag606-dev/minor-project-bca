Our Story

The story of [Golden Fork] started with our founder, [Anurag], who grew up in a family that cherished home-cooked meals and gathered around the table to share stories, laughter, and love.
Inspired by the vibrant culinary traditions passed down through generations, dreamt of creating a space where people could experience the same warmth and joy. With a heart full of dreams and recipes collected from family members, [Anurag, Amit, Ritika Singh] opened the doors of [Golden Fork].

Our Mission

At Our Restaurant, our mission is to serve high-quality meals in a warm and inviting atmosphere. We believe that food is not just about sustenance; it is about bringing people together.
